# CMS Popularity & Open Source Analysis

## 📊 **GitHub Stars & Usage (2025)**

### 🥇 **Most Popular Open Source**
1. **Strapi** ⭐ 63k+ stars
   - ✅ **Fully Open Source** (MIT License)
   - 🌟 **Most widely used** headless CMS
   - 🏢 Used by: NASA, Toyota, IBM, Walmart
   - 💰 Funding: $31M raised, sustainable business model

2. **Sanity.io** ⭐ 5.2k+ stars (core is open source)
   - ⚠️ **Hybrid**: Core open source, hosting is SaaS
   - 🌟 **Very popular** in enterprise
   - 🏢 Used by: Nike, Figma, Netlify, Shopify
   - 💰 Well-funded ($39M), stable company

### 🥈 **Medium Popularity**
3. **TinaCMS** ⭐ 12k+ stars
   - ✅ **Open Source** (Apache 2.0)
   - 🌟 **Growing rapidly** (newer, but gaining traction)
   - 🏢 Used by: Various startups, growing enterprise adoption
   - 💰 VC-backed, focused on developer experience

4. **Decap CMS** ⭐ 17k+ stars
   - ✅ **Fully Open Source** (MIT License)
   - ⚠️ **Declining** (maintenance mode, fewer updates)
   - 🏢 Used by: Many existing sites, but fewer new projects
   - 💰 Community-driven (no company backing)

### 🥉 **Enterprise/Proprietary**
5. **Contentful** - Not open source
   - 💰 **SaaS only** ($28M+ ARR)
   - 🌟 **Very popular** in enterprise
   - 🏢 Used by: Spotify, Urban Outfitters, British Airways

6. **Forestry.io** - Discontinued
   - ❌ **Shut down** (migrated users to Tina)
